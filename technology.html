Technology

DANCING

The combination of specialized technology with the practical knowledge of ballroom dancing gives a product that has never been before.

BLAKE SYSTEM

The construction of the upper of the shoe is stitched with a blake seam along with the midsole and sole.

MOCCASIN SYSTEM

Footwear with an unusual design, where the uppers are not conventionally studded on the hoof.

SEWN-IN SYSTEM

The secret lies in the sewing of the upper in such a way that the shoe has a very high flexibility. In addition, carbon foam placed under the sole, a carbon lining and a Flexible sole were used.

COMFORT ++

A construction developed by us that combines greater shoe thickness with flexibility, and all this is complemented by additional foams that make the shoe very comfortable.

WATERPROOF

A special system of shoe construction and appropriate leathers that ensure water resistance of the shoes.

EXTRA WIDE

Design designed for people with wider feet.

ELEVATOR SHOES

Conhpol elevator shoes with a proprietary insert inside the shoe, which lines the entire length of the shoe
