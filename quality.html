Quality

The materials we use to make our footwear are an element to
which we attach great importance. We use high-quality Polish and
Italian skins (cattle, calf, goat) that meet the standards of European
production, and exotic skins (crocodile, ostrich, kangaroo, python)
with the necessary CITES certificates. They testify to the
authenticity and legality of the sources of their acquisition, and for
us they are a guarantee of the ethics of our activities. The elements
complementing the comfort of use are soles made of leather and raw
materials such as tunite, real rubber and durable, ultra-light EVA
Light material, which complement the comfort of use.
